# Agentic AI Academy


This wiki is internal learning hub for building **deep, durable understanding** of modern AI and software systems—starting from first principles and progressing step by step toward advanced topics like **Agentic AI**.

---

## 🧠 Learning Philosophy

This academy is intentionally **technology‑agnostic** and **concept‑first**.

We focus on **fundamental ideas and mental models** rather than specific libraries, frameworks, or vendors. Technologies evolve quickly; concepts do not. The goal is to help you think clearly and independently, no matter what tools you use.

All material in this academy is designed to be:

- **Beginner‑friendly** — no hidden assumptions or unexplained jumps
- **Concept‑driven** — we explain *why things exist* before *how they are implemented*
- **Layered** — topics build gradually from simple ideas to more advanced ones
- **Durable** — knowledge that remains useful as tools and trends change

Each topic typically progresses through:

> **What is this? → Why does it exist? → How does it work? → When should it be used (or avoided)?**

---

## 🎯 What This Academy Helps You Achieve

By following the learning paths in this wiki, you should be able to:

- Explain core concepts clearly in your own words
- Build strong mental models instead of memorizing recipes
- Recognize patterns that repeat across different technologies
- Make informed design and implementation decisions
- Approach complex systems with confidence

The emphasis is on **understanding and reasoning**, not just usage.
